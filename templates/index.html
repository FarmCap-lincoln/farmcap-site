Why FarmCap?


Agriculture is a necessity globally and it goes without saying that a country
cannot survive without farm yield and agriculture in general.

But unfortunately not enough concern is given to small and average scale farmers,
this has led to continuous increase in market price of farm products.

Fortunately FarmCap is here to help everyday citizens and farmers by bridging the
2 sides to solve the problem of lack of proper funding for farmers and scarcity of
food, where farmers get investments from individual investors and citizens/individual
investors get fresh farm products directly from the source at a lower price.
